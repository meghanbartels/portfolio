Meghan Bartels

Science journalist and editor

I’m currently interning at Nautilus Magazine and editing Scienceline’s environment section.
I’ve previously interned at Tech Insider. I’m also earning a masters in science journalism from New York University’s Science, Health and Environmental Reporting Program and a member of NASW and SEJ.
I am interested in writing, editing, fact checking, and copy editing projects.

In previous lives, I worked at an environmental book publisher and majored in classics at Georgetown University.
I enjoy writing about topics from the environment to infectious diseases, from archaeology to astronomy.
And I have strong feelings about punctuation, of course.
